AlphaReasonAI

Autonomous Reasoning Engine

An autonomous financial advisor agent that reasons through market data — not just reports it. It ingests market signals, financial news, and user portfolios, then builds causal explanations of why your portfolio moved.

Architecture

5-phase pipeline: Data Ingestion → Market Intelligence → Portfolio Analytics → Causal Reasoning → Self-Evaluation

LLM Usage

Hybrid Architecture: Deterministic rule-based causal logic + Dynamic Groq LLM narrative generation.

Data

40 stocks, 5 indices, 25 news articles, 12 mutual funds, 3 portfolio samples, 7-day historical trends

Observability

Langfuse-ready tracing, span-level timing, structured logging, pipeline visualization

Key Capabilities

Causal Linking — Traces News → Sector → Stock → Portfolio impact with confidence scores

Conflict Resolution — Detects when price contradicts news sentiment and explains why

Risk Detection — Flags concentration risk, single-stock exposure, rate sensitivity

Self-Evaluation — Grades its own reasoning on 6 dimensions (A/B/C/D/F)
